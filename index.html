Pbl3-project

This project aims to apply smart city concepts to solve real-world urban problems. Our team is currently exploring different problem areas and evaluating feasible solutions that can be developed into a functional prototype.

Initial Project Ideas

1. Smart Bike Safety Routing

A navigation system that recommends safer routes for cyclists based on road conditions, traffic levels, and bike lane availability, encouraging safer compliance with new cycling regulations.

2. Smart Evacuation Guidance System

A system that helps users find safe evacuation routes during disasters. It focuses on assisting residents, especially foreign users, by providing hazard-aware navigation, evacuation center locations, and emergency guidance.

3. Urban Gardening Assistant

A system that provides plant care recommendations for urban residents based on weather conditions, helping users manage watering, temperature, and plant selection.
